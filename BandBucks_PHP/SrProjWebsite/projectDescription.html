Project Description

So, you and your friends decided to start a band. Congratulations!
But how will you keep track of the money!? Afterall, a band is a business...

Introducing Band Bucks to account for your every need!
Cash, equipment, and debt is all taken care of!
In the mean time you can jam out rather than waste time asking questions like who owes us money? how much cash are we spending on tour? and how much merchandise is on hand?

Band Bucks will track it all for you!

Primary Features:

  • Track cash flows!
  • Track equipment and merchandise!
  • Track who owes what!
  • Generate reports for each!

Are you rolling in the dough?

  • Buying/Selling tickets, merchandise, equipment, food, and gas?
  • One band member owe another?
  • Money split evenly or differently among members?
This is where Band Bucks comes in! This application makes it easier to account for these transactions accurately and consistently.

Cheap and easy accounting!

  • Can't afford an accountant/manager?
  • Perfect for new and upcoming bands!
  • Free web based application!
Band bucks would track this cash and even be able to produce reports or statements from it. This may include, a cash flow statement of cash paid to/from members, or an income statement of how much money the band as a whole has gained or lost within a given time period.

What about the gear?

  • Who owns what?
  • How much merchandise do you own?
  • What is your monetary worth in assets?
A band may also want to track the monetary value of their assets such as special show lights, speakers/amplifiers, or instruments.

Band = Business

  • Track fiscal success/failure!
  • Calendar to track gigs!
  • Contacts tab so no one is left behind!
Every band is a business, and that is one of the essential lessons to learn in the music industry. This software would help take the hassle out of it and allow bands to track their success or failure easily while allowing them to focus on their musical creativity.
